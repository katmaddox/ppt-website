What we review

A thorough, data-backed analysis

Our opinion of value is not a guess or a formula. It's a comprehensive review of your practice across six dimensions — delivered in a clear written report within 5 business days.

01

Revenue & collections

Trailing 12-month and 3-year collections trends, production vs. collections gap, and growth rate analysis.

02

Profitability & overhead

EBITDA, adjusted EBITDA, overhead ratio, and doctor compensation — normalized for market comparison.

03

Patient base quality

Active patient count, recall rate, new patient flow, and concentration risk (single-payer dependence).

04

Physical plant & equipment

Facility condition, lease terms and options, equipment age and functionality, and expansion capacity.

05

Staff & operations

Team stability, key-person dependency, workflow efficiency, and software/technology assessment.

06

Market comparables

Recent sale prices for comparable practices in your specialty, region, and revenue tier — from our proprietary transaction database.

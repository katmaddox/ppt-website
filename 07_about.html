Our Story

Built by practitioners who
understood the stakes

Practice Transition Partners was founded in 2004 by a dentist and a healthcare attorney who had each navigated the practice transition process themselves — and found it opaque, stressful, and underserved. They set out to change that.

Over two decades, we've grown into one of the most trusted practice brokerage firms in the country, with a team of credentialed advisors, in-house valuation specialists, and dedicated transaction coordinators who guide buyers and sellers from first conversation to closed deal.

We've closed transactions in 35+ states, across every major dental and medical specialty. Our average client stays with us because their colleagues refer us — not because we advertise. That word-of-mouth reputation is something we guard fiercely.

Our Values

How we do what we do

These aren't aspirational statements. They're the operating principles behind every transaction we touch.

Confidentiality above all

Your identity, your financials, and your plans stay private. Every prospective buyer executes an NDA before receiving any details. We've never had a confidentiality breach in 22 years.

Transparency at every step

We tell you what a practice is worth — not what you want to hear. Our valuations are independent, data-backed, and fully documented so you can make decisions with confidence.

Aligned incentives

We earn our fee when you close — not when you list. That alignment means we work to get you to a successful transaction, not just to have you on our books.

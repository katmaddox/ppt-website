About this practice

This well-established general dentistry practice has served the Phoenix metro area since 2008. Located in a high-visibility medical office building with ample parking, the practice benefits from a loyal patient base with a strong recall rate and minimal PPO dependency.

The seller is a single-doctor owner looking to retire after 18 years of practice ownership. The transition includes a full seller assist period of up to 90 days to ensure continuity of care and patient retention.

All equipment is modern, fully owned, and in excellent condition. The practice operates on a 4-day week with significant room to expand hours and production.

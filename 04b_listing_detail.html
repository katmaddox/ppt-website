Listing Summary

12 Operatories 3,320 Square Feet

This well-established Pediatric and Orthodontic practice in Fresno offers a rare opportunity to acquire a thriving, community-based dental office with over 40 years of history. Established in 1983, the practice has earned a strong reputation for high-quality care in a fun, welcoming environment — reflected in its 4.5-star Google rating and large, multi-generational patient base.

Ideally located in a highly visible professional building with easy access from the Yosemite Freeway, the practice features excellent signage and ample parking. The 3,322 sq. ft. office includes 12 full-equipped operatories, providing substantial capacity for growth and increased production — truly positioning it as a preferred dental powerhouse.

The practice is equipped with modern technology, including digital X-rays, intraoral cameras, digital impression scanners, and PracticeWorks management software. The facility is in immaculate condition with no deferred maintenance. There is clear opportunity for a buyer looking to expand services or optimize operations.

The seller offers flex real estate and plans to offer a fair lease, creating long-term stability and a smooth transition condition. This is an ideal turnkey opportunity for a pediatric dentist, orthodontist, or group seeking a growth-oriented practice in a strong Central California market.
